Penguin Species How many types of penguins are there? There are currently 18 recognized species of penguins around the world, as determined by various agencies, scientists and experts in the field. Penguins live in many locations around the Southern Hemisphere. At very quick glance they look alike with white and black bodies, but they all have unique characteristics.
